Our Story

A Mumbai Legacy
Since 1940

Nestled in the heart of Charni Road, SGSB – Shri Ganesh Sadanand Bhuvan is more than a restaurant. It is a living heritage, a place where generations of Mumbai families have shared meals, celebrations, and memories since 1940.

Our 100% pure vegetarian kitchen draws on the rich culinary traditions of South India, North India, and the beloved Maharashtrian street food culture — all under one roof.
